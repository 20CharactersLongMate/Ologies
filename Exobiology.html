Exobiology

  • The study of life on other planets or moons
  • The study of the potential for life to exist beyond Earth
  • The study of the conditions and limits of life in extreme environments
  • The study of the effects of space travel and space environments on living organisms
  • The search for biomarkers and other indicators of past or present life on other planets or moons
  • The study of the potential for panspermia, or the transfer of life from one celestial body to another
  • The study of the potential for terraforming, or the transformation of other celestial bodies into habitable environments for human life
  • The study of the potential for space colonization and the establishment of permanent settlements on other celestial bodies
  • The study of the potential for extraterrestrial intelligence and the search for extraterrestrial life
  • The study of the effects of cosmic radiation on living organisms
  • The study of the effects of microgravity on living organisms
  • The study of the effects of space debris and other hazards on living organisms
  • The study of the physiological and psychological effects of long-term space travel on humans
  • The study of the impact of space exploration on the environment and ecosystems of other celestial bodies
  • The study of the potential for the development of space-based agriculture and food production
  • The study of the potential for the use of space resources, such as water and minerals, for human benefit
  • The study of the potential for the development of space-based renewable energy sources
  • The study of the potential for the use of space-based platforms and technologies for Earth observation and monitoring
  • The study of the potential for space-based technologies and platforms to support communication and navigation systems
  • The study of the potential for space-based technologies and platforms to support scientific research and exploration.
