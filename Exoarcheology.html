Exoarcheology

  • The study of ancient life on other planets or moons
  • The search for evidence of past life on other planets or moons
  • The analysis of potential biosignatures on other planets or moons
  • The study of the conditions necessary for the development of life on other planets or moons
  • The comparison of the evolution of life on Earth with the potential evolution of life on other planets or moons
  • The exploration and study of the possibility of terraforming other planets or moons to make them habitable for humans
  • The study of the potential for cross-contamination between Earth and other planets or moons
  • The investigation of the potential for panspermia, or the transfer of life between planets or moons
  • The study of the potential for the formation of life on comets, asteroids, or other small celestial bodies
  • The analysis of the potential for the existence of extraterrestrial civilizations
  • The investigation of the potential for the presence of subsurface oceans or lakes on other planets or moons
  • The study of the geological processes and conditions on other planets or moons
  • The examination of the potential for the presence of microbial life in extreme environments on other planets or moons
  • The analysis of the potential for the presence of biomarkers in the atmospheres of other planets or moons
  • The study of the potential for the presence of organics on other planets or moons
  • The investigation of the potential for the formation of complex organic molecules on other planets or moons
  • The study of the potential for the presence of water ice or other volatile substances on other planets or moons
  • The examination of the potential for the existence of prebiotic chemistry on other planets or moons
  • The analysis of the potential for the presence of life in the subsurface or interior of other planets or moons
  • The study of the potential for the existence of life in the atmospheres of other planets or moons.
